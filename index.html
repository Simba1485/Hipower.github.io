Infrastructure

SUPERGAS has developed a robust infrastructure comprising import terminals, filling plants, and depots at strategic locations to assure uninterrupted, quick, and timely delivery of LPG. 
Our infrastructure consists of two import terminals, 16 filling plants, four depots, a dedicated fleet of delivery vehicles, and over 400 strong franchisees, along with access to additional contracted facilities across India. We serve more than 2,000 industrial customers, over 15,000 commercial customers, more than 1 lakh domestic customers, and operate 50+ Auto LPG dispensing stations.
